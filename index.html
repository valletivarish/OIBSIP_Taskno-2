Biography

Sushant Singh Rajput, born on January 21, 1986, in Bihar, India, initially pursued Mechanical Engineering at the Delhi College of Engineering, achieving a commendable All India Rank (AIR) in the AIEEE. Despite academic success, his passion for acting led him to make a bold decision to drop out and pursue a career in the entertainment industry.

In the world of acting, Sushant rose to fame with his role as Manav Deshmukh in the popular TV series "Pavitra Rishta." Transitioning to Bollywood, he made his debut with the critically acclaimed film "Kai Po Che!" in 2013. Sushant's versatile performances in movies like "Shuddh Desi Romance," "PK," and "Detective Byomkesh Bakshy!" showcased his acting prowess.

A standout moment in his career was the portrayal of Indian cricketer Mahendra Singh Dhoni in "M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story," earning him widespread acclaim. Beyond acting, Sushant was known for his diverse interests, including entrepreneurship and technology.
